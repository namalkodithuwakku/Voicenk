Add these to .env.local and Vercel:

NEXT_PUBLIC_VAPID_PUBLIC_KEY=
VAPID_PRIVATE_KEY=
VAPID_SUBJECT=mailto:your-email@example.com
PUSH_WEBHOOK_SECRET=
SUPABASE_SECRET_KEY=

Existing variables must remain unchanged:

NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_URL=
NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY=
NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL=
OPENAI_API_KEY=

Security:
- NEXT_PUBLIC_VAPID_PUBLIC_KEY is public.
- VAPID_PRIVATE_KEY is server-only.
- PUSH_WEBHOOK_SECRET is server-only.
- SUPABASE_SECRET_KEY is server-only.
- Never prefix private values with NEXT_PUBLIC_.
